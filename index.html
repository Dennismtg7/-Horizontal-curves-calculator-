ABOUT

Built by a Surveying Student

SurveyPro was built by a land surveying & road engineering student at JKUAT — out of genuine frustration with how hard it is to find clean, reliable tools for setting-out work in the field.

Every calculator here has been tested against real coursework problems. The tools are free to use, the formulas are transparent, and everything exports to formats you can actually use in the field or in a report.
